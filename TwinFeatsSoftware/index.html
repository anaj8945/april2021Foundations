About Us

Twin brothers Kent & Kevin Smotherman founded TwinFeats Software in 1983 with a simple premise: Free software for a free world. From the beginings on the Commodore 64 to today's Android apps, that vision has never wavered.

Kent Smotherman has 38 years of professional development experience usng over a dozen programming languages. He enjoys teaching web development, chess, and petting the family cat, Solo (obviously), though he is not a Bond villain.
Kevin Smotherman has 30 years of professional developmen experience. His current role is a Lead/Advisory Software Development Engineer at CSG Intermational. His favorite day of the year is Groundhog Day. No one knows why.
